My creations

Business Analyst YT channel

YouTube channel in pt-br with videos made by me to teach business analysis for people starting to work in the field or who want to up their game.

Sample Size Calculator

Online calculator for finding the minimum quantity of observations for your test to be statistically significant. Unlike other online calculators, this one guides the user to the right interpretation of the results in the form of a well-structured phrase.

Easy CHAID

Web application for creating classification trees using CHAID (Chi-squared Automatic Interaction Detection) algorithm, the same one used by IBM's SPSS software. Easy CHAID was developed by me in Javascript.

Table Join Cheat Sheet

Have you ever found yourself trying to remember the syntax to join two [or more] tables? Keep this cheat sheet handy on your favorites bookmark or printed out near your workstation for a quick reference.

Comparison of online stat. sig. calculators

A large table comparing the most popular online calculators for sample size, statistical significance, confidence interval and other metrics related to testing.
